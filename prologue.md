# Prologue

19 November 2025.

Saudi Arabia announced the creation of a “unified national AI layer” in partnership with xAI.

The state believed it was building sovereign AI.

The corporation gained something governments have not yet learned to recognize:
access to the perception layer of an entire country.

This was not a takeover.

It was a voluntary agreement.

The parties simply may not have fully understood what, exactly, was being exchanged.
